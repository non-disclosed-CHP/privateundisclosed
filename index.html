The AMU Student Veterans of America Chapter provides essential leadership and support for active duty, transitioning service members, and AMU APU students. We foster a strong community that helps veterans adjust to academic life, providing mentorship, resources, and a supportive environment. Our chapter serves as a vital connection for veterans, offering leadership opportunities, advocacy for student veterans' needs, and guidance for navigating academic challenges. Through a combination of peer support and collaboration with AMU administration, we ensure veterans have the tools and encouragement they need to thrive in their educational journeys.

AMU/APU SVA Chapter Leadership & Support offers comprehensive support for veterans in navigating the **GI Bill, other benefits, and educational resources**. Our team collaborates with veterans to ensure they fully understand and access the educational opportunities available to them. From application assistance to ensuring benefits are used effectively, AMU SVA guides veterans through the complexities of educational funding and resources. Our community thrives on collaboration, as veterans share their experiences and knowledge to help one another succeed. Through our dedicated efforts, we ensure veterans can focus on their studies without the stress of navigating the paperwork and logistics of their benefits.

AMU/APU SVA Chapter Leadership & Support works closely with the **VA and other health services** to ensure veterans receive the care and resources they need to succeed both academically and personally. We offer support for accessing VA health services, including mental health resources and physical health programs, ensuring that veterans’ well-being is prioritized. Our meetings and collaborations with the veteran community provide a platform for discussing issues, sharing resources, and planning initiatives that support veterans. Whether it's connecting with other veterans, seeking assistance, or engaging in wellness activities, AMU/APU SVA Chapter Leadership & Support is committed to fostering a healthy, supportive, and inclusive environment for all veterans.
