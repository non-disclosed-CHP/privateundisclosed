AMU-APU SVA's Legacy Project - Veteran Stories

Preserving Veterans' Stories for Future Generations

Our Mission

The Legacy Project is dedicated to honoring the service and sacrifice of our veteran community by collecting and archiving their unique stories. By sharing these personal histories, we aim to bridge the civilian-military divide, provide valuable educational resources, and ensure the rich experiences of our service members are never forgotten.

This space will eventually showcase individual written and video narratives.

Want to Get Involved?

Help us collect, record, and share veteran stories. Volunteer, submit a story, or spread the word.
